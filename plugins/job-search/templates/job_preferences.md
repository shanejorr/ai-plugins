## Hard requirements

A role must meet ALL of these or it is dropped:

- **Location:** e.g. fully remote / remote-US / hybrid within 30 miles of Atlanta, GA
- **Function:** e.g. data science, data analyst, AI engineer
- **Seniority:** e.g. roles asking for 5+ years experience

## Strong preferences

Rank matching roles higher, but don't disqualify:

- **Sector:** e.g. nonprofit / mission-driven, law firm, government
- **Industry:** e.g. education, economic development, criminal justice
- **Org size:** e.g. under 100 employees preferred, any size acceptable
- **Schedule:** e.g. part-time preferred, full-time acceptable
- **Salary:** e.g. minimum, target range
